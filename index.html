Frequently Asked Questions

Essential items to carry:

  • Comfortable trekking shoes (mandatory)
  • 2 liters of water
  • Personal snacks (energy bars, dry fruits)
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Light jacket or windcheater
  • Personal medications if any
  • Torch/flashlight
  • Small backpack (20-30L)
  • Extra pair of clothes
  • ID proof (mandatory)

Most of our adventures are moderate-level treks. You should be able to:

  • Walk 8-10 km comfortably
  • Climb stairs without getting too breathless
  • Have no major knee or back issues

If you've done basic hikes or treks before, you'll be fine! First-timers with decent fitness can also complete the trek comfortably.

Yes! Your safety is our top priority. Here's what we ensure:

  • Experienced local guides who know the terrain
  • Basic first-aid kit available
  • Small group size (usually 15-20 max) for better supervision
  • We check weather conditions before every batch
  • Trek is cancelled/postponed if conditions are unsafe

Just follow the guide's instructions and you'll have a safe, memorable adventure!

Cancellation Policy:

  • 7+ days before: 80% refund
  • 3-7 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 3 days: No refund
  • You can transfer your booking to someone else at no extra cost

If we cancel due to weather/forest closure, you get a full refund or can reschedule to another date.

Facilities depend on the location. Most base villages have basic washroom facilities. During the trek, there are no dedicated facilities, but the guide will arrange bio-breaks at appropriate spots with natural cover.
